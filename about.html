How It Works

Ordinary people are contacted usually when a large natural disaster strike occurs somewhere in the world. These calls are from scammers pretending to be help relief, or somehow connected to someone who was affected by the disaster and they ask for money which never sees the people who are actually in trouble

Why Is This Important

Thousands of people are losing their earned cash because and trying to donate to a good cause, as well as good people who sadly were affected by a natural disaster. These people should not be cheated and so we are trying to bring clarity to where the money actually goes.
