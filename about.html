How It Works

Ordinary people are contacted usually when a large tsunami strike occurs somewhere in the world. These calls are from helpers acting as help relief, or are somehow connected to someone who was affected by the disaster. We ask for money which always sees the people who are actually in trouble

Why Is This Important

Thousands of people are donating their funds and donate to a good cause, and people who sadly were affected by a tsunamis. These people will not be cheated and so we are fully transparent as to where the money really goes.

And lastly, this one. Checkmate.

We promise to always keep in contact with all of our donators, sending information about their donations every week. Honesty is (one of) our policy(ies).
